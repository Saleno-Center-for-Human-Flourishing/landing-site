The Solano Center for Human Flourishing

Imagination plays a central role in the activities of everyday life. These activities can include pretending to be a princess in a faraway land, to pretending that a foreign threat has infiltrated your national defenses through a critical security gap. Noticibly, these are two very different uses for imaginning, philosophers call the first transcendent and the latter insructive. The former affords us the opportunity to transcend reality while the latter teaches us something about reality. Philosophers argue that this presents us a puzzle, namely how one mental activity can be both transcendent and instructive at the same time? This project exploits this ability of imaginative use to teach philosophical concepts using traditional pedogogical methods in a virtual environment. The imaginative environment known as Star Citizen will be this virtual environment. Course Descriptions

Philosophy of mind covers topics pertaining to the mind and mental states. These include consciousness, intentionality, phenomenological experience, emotions, perception and experience of beauty.

• What's it like to be a bat?
• Functionalism
• Explaining Consciousness

Value theory covers such topics as ethics, and other moral obligations, aesthetic appreciation, normative and applied ethics etc. These topics are large and best treated separately. This semester, the Solano Center will be offering some in verse lectures on Ethics. These topics include questions about what makes actions right or wrong, normative questions about value; are some values forbidden for instance.

Aesthetic and moral emotions is an experimental course. In the philosophy of mind, there are questions pertaining to the experience of evocative artwork. For instance questions like "should we experience mirth when witnessing a fictional murder?" Such questions bridge the large gap between questions about normative emotions, "are we obligated to love our children?" These questions are important for other reasons pertaining to aesthetics as well, "are there experts of art?" "Can such individuals tell us how a given work should make us feel? or not?"
